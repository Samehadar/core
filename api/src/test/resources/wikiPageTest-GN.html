Visa requirements for Guinean citizens

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Visa requirements for Guinean citizens are administrative entry restrictions by the authorities of other states placed on citizens of the Guinea. As of 1 January 2017, Guinean citizens had visa-free or visa on arrival access to 48 countries and territories, ranking the Guinean passport 88th in terms of travel freedom (tied with Algerian, Cambodian, Comorian, Egyptian and Laotian passports) according to the Henley visa restrictions index.[1]
